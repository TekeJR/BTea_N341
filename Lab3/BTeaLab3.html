Ramen Selection

Garlic Tonkatsu
- A rich ramen made with pork and chicken bone broth simmered over 12 hours per day. Served traditionally with garlic, pork belly, seaweed, moyashi and green onions.
Tonkatsu
- A rich, classic ramen that consists of bone broth, pork belly, moyashi as well as green onion.
Shoyu
- A light ramen made with pork bones simmered for several hours. It captures a soy flavor with pork belly, seaweed, yasai, and green onion.
Shio
- A light ramen made with pork bones simmered for several hours. Less soy flavor with pork belly, seaweed, yasai, and green onion.
Miso
- A heavy ramen crafted with awase miso paste that combines tonkatsu pork broth with light pork/chicken broth to make a savor flavor. Served with corn, moyashi, and menma.

Izakaya

    Chicken Karaage

    Pork Gyoza

    Takoyaki
    - Ball-shaped food made of wheat flour-based batter with octopus filling, topped with bonito flakes, seaweed powder, okonomi sauce, and japanese mayonnaise.
